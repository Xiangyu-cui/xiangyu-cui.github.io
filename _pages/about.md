<section class="content-section">
  <h2>Selected Work</h2>
  <div class="timeline-list">
    <article>
      <p class="item-meta">2025 - 2026 | NYU Abu Dhabi</p>
      <h3>Channel Power Gain Analysis and Orientation Optimization for CAPA Systems</h3>
      <p>Derived semi-closed-form near-field channel power gain expressions for continuous aperture arrays and studied how aperture orientation, size, distance, and misalignment affect received gain.</p>
    </article>
    <article>
      <p class="item-meta">2026 | IEEE Wireless Communications Letters</p>
      <h3>Channel Capacity Saturation Point and Beamforming Acceleration for Near-Field XL-MIMO</h3>
      <p>Investigated capacity saturation in near-field XL-MIMO multiuser systems and proposed a low-complexity beamforming method near the saturation point.</p>
    </article>
    <article>
      <p class="item-meta">2024 | IEEE Open Journal of the Communications Society</p>
      <h3>Near-Field Analysis of Extremely Large-Scale MIMO</h3>
      <p>Developed received-power and correlation analysis for near-field channel models and applied the results to faster user selection in XL-MIMO uplink systems.</p>
    </article>
  </div>
